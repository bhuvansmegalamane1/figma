Our Mission

Built on honest work, not just specs

Mangalam started in 1996 with a simple goal: make pipes that don't leak and deliver them on time. That's still the goal.

We now run a 500,000 sq.ft plant and ship to 40+ countries. Our R&D team works on real problems — longer coil runs, better joint strength, pressure ratings for Indian soil conditions.
